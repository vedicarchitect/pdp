Rules for selling

VIX not spike > 5% for the day 
VIX should not be at day high
VIX should not increase in the last 3 5m candles, it should be decreasing or stable for 15

1 hour alignment
 - if price above 50 ema, emas are aligned 9,20,50 its bullish bias
 - if price below 50 ema, emas are aligned 9,20,50 its bearish bias

15 min alignment
 - if price above 50 ema, emas are aligned 9,20,50 its bullish bias
 - if price below 50 ema, emas are aligned 9,20,50 its bearish bias

5 min 
 - price should be above 50 ema for bullish bias
 - price should be below 50 ema for bearish bias
 
 Camrilla pivot Daily
  - if price break above R3 or R4 with candle close above it then bias is bullish
  - if price break below R3 or R4 with candle close below it then bias is bearish

Camrilla pivot Weekly
  - if price break above R3 or R4 with candle close above it then bias is bullish
  - if price break below R3 or R4 with candle close below it then bias is bearish

Swing high & low
 if price close above PDH,PWH then bullish
 if price close below PDH,PWL then bearish

Vwap 
 - when vwap low is broken bearish
 - when vwap high is broken bullish

 ORB
  - when 15m orb broken above by 15m candle then bullish
  - when 15m orb broken below by 15m candle then bearish
PCR 
  if pcr above 1.1, then its bullish
  if pcr below 0.9, then its bearish

 Entry Rules:
  - Additional Bearish entry allowed after R3 or R4 rejection or 50 ema or 20 ema or 9ema  with upper wick candle from above (resistance)
  - Additional Bullish entry allowed after S3 or S4 breakout or 50 ema or 20 ema or 9ema  with lower wick candle from below (support)
 -  Entry after 10.15 1 hour candle complete  
Adjustment or change of trend
 - When 15m 50 ema broken or crossed 9ema and 20 ema in opposite direction then adjust or change of trend
 - When 1h 50 ema broken or crossed 9ema and 20 ema in opposite direction then adjust or change of trend


Option selling with directional strangle strategy:

Based on bias, enter into option selling

  Strike selection:
   - if bullish bias is strong choose 0.6 delta and above based on bias strength or choose strikes near R3 or R4 or Supertrend resistance having premium more than 50 
   - if bearish bias is strong choose 0.6 delta and above based on bias strength or choose strikes near S3 or S4 or Supertrend support having premium more than 50 
   Based on bullish and bearish, after 1 hour candle complete, create strangle - 
   - if more bullish prefer sell more pe and less ce 3 pe: 2ce
    -most bullish 4pe:2ce

   Complete bullish - sell ATM pe - 5pe
   Bearish
   - most bearish 2pe:4ce
   - more bearish 2pe:3ce
   complete bearish - sell ATM ce - 5ce

   Rollup - when premium < 20
   Exit when trend getting strong one side and when premium is doubled or major support or resistance broken

   Exit when loss > 15000 for the day
