## Why

The events backend publishes realtime events but the app has no sidebar feed to surface them (e.g. 'NIFTY crossed 50 EMA 1H').

A configurable realtime event feed in the app sidebar on /api/v1/events + /ws: per-instrument selection and configurable event types.
